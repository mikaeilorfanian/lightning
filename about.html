About

I started programming in C at the age of 13 but moved on to other hobbies until I was 25. In college, I studied economics, finance, philosophy, English Literature, and Computer Science. As for work, I've held positions as Backend Engineer, UI Designer, UX Researcher, Product Owner, and Data Engineer.
My main focus outside work is on answering questions like:

  • How can the tools used by developers be improved so that we're more productive?
  • What defines a good developer and how can one become a top 1% developer?
